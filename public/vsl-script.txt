=== FILMING NOTES ===

Shooting order (easiest to hardest energy):
1. Offer section (just listing things, low pressure)
2. Credibility (your story, you know it)
3. Problem (needs intensity)
4. Objection handling (conversational)
5. CTA (short, confident)
6. Hook (film last when warmed up, most important 15 seconds)

Setup:
- iPhone on tripod, eye level, landscape 4K
- Lav mic or AirPods Pro (audio matters more than video)
- Face a window for natural light, or ring light behind camera
- Cleanest corner of dorm, plain wall or desk setup
- Head and upper chest in frame, slightly off-center
- Look at the camera lens, not the screen
- Stand up even if only upper body is in frame

Editing:
- Jump cuts are fine, cut out pauses and ums
- Light background music at 10-15% volume
- Bump contrast, lower highlights, add slight warmth
- Export 1080p minimum, 4K if possible
- Name file vsl.mp4, drop in /public
